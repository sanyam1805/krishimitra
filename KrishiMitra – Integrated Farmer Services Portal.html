Module 7

🏛️ Government Schemes for Farmers

Explore central and state government schemes with eligibility criteria and application process

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

PM-KISAN

Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year in 3 equal installments to small and marginal farmer families.

💰 Benefits: ₹2,000 per installment (3 times/year)

Eligibility:

  • Landholding ≤ 2 hectares
  • Family members: farmer, spouse, minor children
  • Age ≥ 18 years

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

PMFBY

Comprehensive crop insurance coverage against crop loss due to natural calamities and adverse weather.

🛡️ Coverage: Up to ₹1,50,000 per claim

Premium Rates (Approx):

  • Kharif: 2% of Sum Insured
  • Rabi: 1.5% of Sum Insured
  • Summer: 5% of Sum Insured

PM Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

PMKSY

Subsidized micro-irrigation systems including drip and sprinkler irrigation for efficient water use.

💧 Subsidy: 50-90% depending on state

Eligible Systems:

  • Drip irrigation systems
  • Sprinkler irrigation
  • Farm pond construction

Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization

SMAM

Subsidies for purchase of agricultural machinery and equipment to improve farm productivity.

🚜 Subsidy: 25-50% on machinery cost

Covered Equipment:

  • Tractors & Power tillers
  • Harvesters & Threshers
  • Seed drills & Planters

National Food Security Mission

NFSM

Subsidized seeds, soil nutrients, and extension services for wheat, rice, and pulses production.

🌾 Subsidy: Certified seeds at ₹20-40/kg

Focus Crops:

  • Wheat & Rice
  • Pulses (Arhar, Gram, Lentil)
  • Coarse cereals

Soil Health Card Scheme

SHC

Free soil testing and nutrient recommendations for optimal crop productivity and sustainability.

🧪 Cost: Free testing & card issuance

Services Included:

  • Soil nutrient testing
  • pH & Organic matter analysis
  • Fertilizer recommendations
